About us

Welcome to MediTour, where innovation meets healthcare to redefine the patient experience.

At MediTour, we are committed to leveraging cutting-edge Health Tech Facility Mapping technology to offer users a virtual tour of hospital rooms. Our mission is to enhance the overall healthcare journey for patients and visitors alike by providing a comprehensive and interactive guide to hospital facilities. With a focus on seamless navigation, familiarity, and comfort, we strive to alleviate the stress and anxiety often associated with hospital visits. Join us on this transformative journey as we revolutionize the way individuals interact with healthcare environments, one virtual tour at a time.
